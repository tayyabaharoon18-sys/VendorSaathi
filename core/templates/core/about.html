About VendorSaathi

Empowering Street Vendors, Building Communities

Our Mission

VendorSaathi is dedicated to empowering street vendors across India by providing them with a digital platform to showcase their products and services to a wider audience.

We believe that every street vendor has a unique story and deserves recognition for their hard work and entrepreneurship. Our platform bridges the gap between traditional street vendors and modern consumers, creating opportunities for growth and sustainability.

Through technology and community support, we aim to transform the informal economy and provide vendors with the tools they need to succeed in the digital age.

Our Vision

To create a thriving ecosystem where every street vendor in India has access to digital tools, fair opportunities, and a supportive community.

We envision a future where street vending is recognized as a legitimate and valued form of entrepreneurship, contributing significantly to local economies and preserving cultural heritage.

Digital Inclusion: Bringing street vendors into the digital economy
Economic Growth: Creating sustainable income opportunities
Community Building: Fostering connections between vendors and customers

Our Story

The people behind VendorSaathi

VendorSaathi was born from a simple observation: street vendors are the backbone of India's informal economy, yet they often lack visibility and access to modern business tools.

Our founders, passionate about social impact and technology, came together with a shared vision to create a platform that could level the playing field for street vendors. We started in Lucknow, India, working closely with local vendors to understand their challenges and needs.

What began as a small initiative has grown into a vibrant platform connecting thousands of vendors with customers across India. Every day, we're inspired by the stories of resilience, creativity, and entrepreneurship from the vendors on our platform.
